# Running the Giveaway

## Before the livestream

1. Confirm all three n8n workflows are active.
2. Confirm the registration webhook is receiving entrants.
3. Confirm the correct Zoom meeting requires registration.
4. Confirm all creator-team members are in the `staff` table.
5. Confirm a test entrant can join with their personal Zoom link.
6. Confirm a join and leave event reaches Workflow 2.
7. Confirm the private dashboard can read live status.
8. Confirm the Google credential can create a spreadsheet.
9. Agree on who will click **Lock Giveaway List**.
10. Agree on a manual fallback before the stream begins.

## During the livestream

1. Keep the private dashboard open.
2. Watch the counts, especially **Eligible now**, **Staff connected**, and **Unmatched**.
3. When it is time for the giveaway, announce that the list is about to close.
4. Pause briefly so the latest join and leave events can be processed.
5. Click **Lock Giveaway List** once.
6. Confirm the page shows the final eligible count and range.
7. Open the generated Google Sheet.
8. Share the sheet and the browser random-number generator on-screen as appropriate.
9. Set the generator minimum to `1`.
10. Set the maximum to the eligible count shown by the system.
11. Generate one number.
12. Search that draw number in the locked sheet.
13. Confirm the named person is still able to respond under the creator's stated rules.
14. Record the winner and their chosen prize.

## Important operating rules

- Do not use the original registration range after the eligible list is locked.
- Do not manually delete or rearrange rows in the locked sheet.
- Do not create a new snapshot because someone dislikes the result.
- Do not show full attendee emails on-stream.
- Do not include unmatched guests manually unless the giveaway rules explicitly allow a documented correction.

## After the livestream

1. Save the lock ID, sheet URL and winner record.
2. Restrict the final sheet to the people who need it.
3. Review unmatched attendance only if a dispute requires it.
4. Delete unique join URLs and raw event data when they are no longer needed under the team's retention policy.
5. Record any problems before the next event.
