# Demo Video Script

Target: 90 seconds.

## 0:00–0:12 — Problem

> The original giveaway spreadsheet showed who registered, but it could not show who was still inside the Zoom meeting. That meant the random generator could select people who had already left.

Show the project title and the empty live-status screen.

## 0:12–0:28 — Seed and join

> This system keeps the creator's current public draw process. It adds live attendance qualification behind it.

Run `DEMO — Reset & Seed 25 Entrants`, then `DEMO 1 — Join 25 Entrants`.

## 0:28–0:42 — Staff exclusion

> The creator, moderator and producer can remain on the call, but the system identifies them as staff and excludes them from the draw.

Run `DEMO 2 — Join 3 Staff` and show that the eligible count does not rise.

## 0:42–0:55 — Departures

> When five entrants leave before the giveaway, the eligible count falls automatically.

Run `DEMO 3 — Five Entrants Leave`.

## 0:55–1:12 — Lock

> At giveaway time, the creator locks one permanent snapshot. The eligible entrants are sorted by their original serial and assigned temporary numbers from one to the exact total.

Use the dashboard or run `DEMO — Lock & Create Google Sheet`.

## 1:12–1:25 — Public draw

> The creator still shares a normal browser random-number generator. Every possible number now maps to a registered attendee who was present, while staff and absent people are removed.

Show the range `1–20`, generate one number, and find it in the locked sheet.

## 1:25–1:30 — Close

> The selection stays public. The automation makes the eligible list accurate and auditable.
